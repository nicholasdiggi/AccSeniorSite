Welcome to SiteTrainer

Our website is designed to help people learn how to navigate websites with ease! We understand that using technology can be overwhelming, especially for those who didn't grow up with it. That's why we created this website with you in mind. Our goal is to provide you with the knowledge and skills you need to confidently and comfortably navigate the web. Our website is full of helpful tips and step-by-step tutorials on how to use popular websites such as social media platforms, online shopping sites, and news outlets. We've also included simple explanations of technical terms and concepts to help demystify the online world. We believe that everyone should have access to the wealth of information and resources that the internet provides, regardless of their age or level of technological expertise. With our website, you'll be able to enjoy all that the web has to offer with confidence and ease. Thank you for visiting our website, and we hope that you find it helpful and informative.

The Navigation Bar

The navigation bar is a menu that helps you move around a website. It usually appears at the top or side of the screen and contains clickable links or buttons that take you to different pages or sections of the website. It helps you find what you're looking for easily without having to search for it on your own.

Home

On SiteTrainer, the home button takes you to the front page where you will be able to find information about SiteTrainer.

About

The about button takes you the page that tells more about the developers of SiteTrainer.

Search Bar

The search bar lets you search for specific things within SiteTrainer. When you type in what you're looking for, it shows you a list of results that match your search.

Contact

The contact button takes you to the webpage that allows you to find the contact information of the creators of SiteTrainer.
